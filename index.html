Sovereign Gold Bond

Government of India has decided to float Sovereign Gold Bond (SGB) with an objective to shift physical gold into demat gold bonds.The main objective of SGB project is
 To handle Investor registration in portal by agency banks.
 To handle SGB subscription in portal by agency banks.
 To handle Investor modification in portal.
 To handle Subscription modification in portal.
 To generate Subscription acknowledgment report.
My contribution in the project was to develop Investor registration page. Tools/Platforms: Java 1.6, Eclipse IDE, PL/SQL Developer.
